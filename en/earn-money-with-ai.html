Frequently Asked Questions – Earning Money with AI

How can I start earning money online with AI in 2026?

The fastest ways to earn online with AI include: selling digital products on Gumroad, freelancing commission-free on Contra, creating UGC content through Klipujme or Twirl, or finding remote jobs through Remote.co. Most platforms are free to join.

What is UGC and how do I earn from it?

UGC (User-Generated Content) is authentic video content creators make for brand marketing campaigns. Platforms like Klipujme, Twirl, and The UGC Agency pay creators for making product videos. You don't need a large following — just a smartphone and creativity.

How do I sell digital products online?

Gumroad is the simplest platform to sell digital products like ebooks, courses, and templates. Create an account, upload your product, set a price, and share your link. Gumroad handles payments and delivery automatically.
